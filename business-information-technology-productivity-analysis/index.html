Productivity Growth

productivity growth = productivity at t+1 / productivity at t

Efficiency

efficiency = output produced / output maximum

  • aways a ratio or percentage, 0 <= e < 1
Efficiency Change

efficiency change = efficiency at t+1 / efficiency at t

  • always an index, 0 <= ec <= 1

Technical Change

Production frontier is the line that specifies the ideal maximum output produced at any input level

technical change = slope of production frontier at t+1 / slope of production frontier at t

  • always an index, 0 <= pf <= 1

Productivity Growth = Efficiency Change * Technical Change
